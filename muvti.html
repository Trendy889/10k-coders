Maa Oori Polimera, the first movie, was released on the internet and got a lot of love. Now, a new movie, Maa Oori Polimera 2, is coming. It is about magic.

This new movie is a sequel to the first one. It will talk about magic and science

They showed a video of the new movie on Friday. The video starts with someone on TV asking if real magic is real.

The video looks great, and the music is scary. The new movie looks like it cost more money to make than the first one. Other movies like Kantara, Karthikeya 2, and Virupaksha are liked by people. The people who made this movie think people will like it too.
